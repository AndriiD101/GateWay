About our project and team

GateWay is a university project created by a team of students. We wanted to tackle a real-world problem: to make travel planning quick and enjoyable using modern web technologies and artificial intelligence.

Our work process

We started by brainstorming and defining the core features of our app. Then we divided the work based on our strengths: Vladyslav focused on frontend development, Danil handled AI integration, and Andrii managed the project and testing. We used agile methodologies to stay organized and ensure smooth collaboration throughout the development process.
